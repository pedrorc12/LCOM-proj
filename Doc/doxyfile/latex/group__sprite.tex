Animate the sprite \char`\"{}fig\char`\"{} according to its attributes in memory, whose address is \char`\"{}base\char`\"{}. The animation detects the screen borders and change the speed according; it also detects collision with other objects, including the player pad. Collisions with the screen border generates a perfect reflection, while collision with other objects generates a random perturbation in the object speed. Other strategies can be devised\+: use quasi-\/elastic collision based on the objects \char`\"{}mass\char`\"{} and speed, generate spin effect based on amount of tangential speed direction relative to the object center of \char`\"{}mass\char`\"{}...
